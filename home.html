aboutus
club goal

Our goal is to educate the campus community about the importance of online security and provide them with the skills to protect themselves from potential cyber threats. The club aims to create a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for cybersecurity and work together to enhance their knowledge and skills in this field. The club also provides opportunities for members to network with professionals in the industry and participate in hands-on projects and competitions.
